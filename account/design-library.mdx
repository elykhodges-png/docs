Your Inkbot Design Library is where every piece of artwork you upload or create lives. Whether you're refining a concept in the studio, previewing placement on your body scan, or queuing up a design for your next session, the library gives you fast, organized access to all your work from any device.

## Accessing the library

Open your library from anywhere in the Inkbot app or web dashboard by navigating to **Dashboard > Designs**. All your saved designs load in a grid view, and you can switch to list view using the toggle in the top-right corner.

## Organizing designs

Keeping your library tidy makes it faster to find the right design when you're ready to book. Use the following tools to stay organized:

- **Add tags** — Attach custom tags to any design (e.g. `sleeve`, `back piece`, `flash`, `client request`) to group related artwork together. Tags can be added or removed at any time from the design's detail panel.
- **Search** — Use the search bar at the top of the library to find designs by name or tag. Results update as you type.
- **Sort** — Sort your library by date uploaded (newest or oldest first) or alphabetically by design name using the **Sort** dropdown.
- **Create collections** — Group designs into named collections for more structured organization, such as grouping all designs for a specific body zone or style. **Collections are available on the Pro plan only.**

## Design actions

Click any design in your library to open its detail panel, where you can take the following actions:

| Action | How to access |
|---|---|
| Edit in studio | Click design > **Edit** |
| Create Print Config | Click design > **Preview on Body** |
| Duplicate | Click design > **Duplicate** |
| Download original | Click design > **Download** |
| Delete | Click design > **Delete** (cannot be undone) |

## Storage limits

The number of designs you can save depends on your Inkbot subscription tier.

<Info>
  **Free accounts** can store up to **100 designs**. Once you reach the limit, you'll need to delete existing designs before uploading new ones. **Pro accounts** enjoy **unlimited design storage**, so you never have to choose between keeping old work and adding something new. Upgrade at any time under **Account > Billing > Subscription**.
</Info>

## Sharing designs

You can share any design from your library as a **read-only link**, allowing others to view the artwork without accessing your account. To generate a share link, open the design's detail panel and go to **Settings > Share**, then copy the link and send it however you like.

<Note>
  Shared designs are viewable by anyone with the link but **cannot be printed from another account**. The recipient can see the design artwork only — they cannot add it to their own library, edit it, or use it to book a session.
</Note>
