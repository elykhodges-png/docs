Your Inkbot profile is the central hub for managing everything tied to your account — from personal details and payment methods to notification preferences and your subscription plan. Keep your information up to date to ensure a smooth booking experience every time you visit a studio.

## Account details

Your account details control how Inkbot identifies you and secures your access. To update any of the following, navigate to **Account > Settings > Profile**.

- **Display name** — The name shown on your profile and session confirmations. You can update this at any time.
- **Email address** — Your primary contact and login email. Changing your email address triggers a re-verification email to the new address; your old address remains active until verification is complete.
- **Password** — Your password must be a minimum of 8 characters and include at least one number and one special character (e.g. `!`, `@`, `#`). Update your password under **Account > Settings > Security**.
- **Profile photo** — Upload a profile image (JPEG or PNG, max 5 MB) to personalize your account dashboard.

## Notification preferences

Control exactly which communications Inkbot sends you by navigating to **Account > Settings > Notifications**. Toggle each notification type on or off independently.

- **Booking reminders** — Receive email and/or SMS reminders ahead of your upcoming session. Set your preferred lead time (e.g. 24 hours or 1 hour before).
- **Session confirmations** — Get an instant confirmation via email or SMS whenever a session is booked, rescheduled, or cancelled.
- **Promotional offers** — Opt in to receive news about limited-time pricing, new studio locations, and seasonal design drops.
- **Aftercare reminders** — Receive a follow-up message after each completed session with aftercare instructions and a link to your session report.

## Payment methods

Inkbot accepts the following payment methods for session bookings and Pro subscriptions:

- **Cards** — Visa, Mastercard, and American Express
- **Digital wallets** — Apple Pay and Google Pay

**To add a new payment method:**

1. Go to **Account > Billing > Add Payment Method**.
2. Select your payment type (card or digital wallet).
3. Enter your payment details and confirm.

Your saved payment methods are encrypted and stored securely. You can add multiple methods and designate one as your default for faster checkout.

## Subscription plan

Inkbot offers two tiers to match how often you use the service. Your current plan is always visible under **Account > Billing > Subscription**.

| Feature | Free | Pro |
|---|---|---|
| Saved designs | Up to 100 | Unlimited |
| Body scan saves | 5 | Unlimited |
| Studio tools | Basic | Advanced |
| Priority booking | No | Yes |
| Price | Free | $9.99/mo |

<Tip>
  If you book sessions regularly or work with complex multi-layer designs, upgrading to **Inkbot Pro** unlocks unlimited storage, advanced studio tools like color correction, and priority booking — all for $9.99/mo. Upgrade anytime under **Account > Billing > Subscription**.
</Tip>

## Deleting your account

You can request account deletion at any time from **Account > Settings > Security > Delete Account**. Before submitting a deletion request, consider downloading your designs and session reports, as this data cannot be recovered once your account is removed.

<Warning>
  Account deletion is **permanent**. All saved designs, body scan data, and order history will be immediately and irreversibly removed. This action cannot be undone, and Inkbot support cannot restore deleted accounts or associated data.
</Warning>
