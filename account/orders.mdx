Every session you book through Inkbot is recorded as an order in your account history, giving you a full audit trail of your tattoo journey. From here you can review past sessions, download your session reports, and quickly reprint a design you love without starting from scratch.

## Viewing order history

Access your full session history by navigating to **Dashboard > Orders**. Orders are listed in reverse chronological order, with the most recent session at the top. Each order card displays the following details at a glance:

- **Session date and studio** — The date your session took place and the Inkbot studio location.
- **Design used** — The name and thumbnail of the design that was printed during the session.
- **Body zone** — The targeted area of the body mapped during the session (e.g. left forearm, upper back).
- **Total cost** — The final amount charged for the session, including any applicable fees.
- **Session status** — The current status of the order (see below).

## Order statuses

Each order is assigned a status that reflects where it is in the session lifecycle.

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Scheduled | Booking confirmed, session upcoming |
| In Progress | Session currently active at studio |
| Completed | Session finished successfully |
| Cancelled | Session was cancelled |
| Refunded | Refund processed |

## Session report

After a session reaches **Completed** status, Inkbot generates a PDF session report you can download for your records. To access it, open the order and click **Download Report**.

Each session report contains:

- **Design used** — A reference copy of the artwork applied during the session.
- **Placement coordinates** — The precise 3D body-mapping coordinates used to position the design on your skin.
- **Ink batch info** — The batch number and composition of the ink used, useful for allergy follow-ups or touch-up sessions.
- **Aftercare reminder** — Tailored aftercare instructions based on the body zone and session duration.

## Reprinting a design

Want to reproduce a past design — in the same spot or somewhere new? Reprint it directly from your order history in just a few steps.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Dashboard > Orders">
    Open your session history from the main dashboard navigation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the completed order you want to reprint">
    Scroll through your order history or use the search bar to locate the session. Only orders with a **Completed** status can be reprinted.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Reprint This Design">
    Open the order detail view and click the **Reprint This Design** button to load the original design and its Print Configuration into the booking flow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Adjust placement if needed in the preview tool">
    Review the body zone and placement coordinates in the preview tool. Reposition or rescale the design if you want a different placement for this session.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Book a new session">
    Select your preferred studio, date, and time, then confirm your booking. The new order will appear in your order history with a **Scheduled** status.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Reprint sessions use the **same Print Configuration** as the original order by default, including the exact placement coordinates and scaling. If you adjust the placement in Step 4, a new Print Configuration is saved to your Design Library — your original configuration remains unchanged.
</Info>
