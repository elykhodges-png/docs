Getting your first Inkbot tattoo is straightforward. In just a few minutes online, you can set up your account, upload your artwork, preview how it will look on your body, and lock in a studio appointment — all before you ever step through the door. Follow the four steps below to go from idea to booked session.

<Warning>
  Walk-in sessions are **not available** at any Inkbot studio. All appointments must be booked in advance through your Inkbot account. Same-day bookings may be available at select studios, subject to technician availability.
</Warning>

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Account">
    Go to [inkbot.io/signup](https://inkbot.io/signup) and fill in the registration form:

    - **Full name** — used on your booking confirmation and session records
    - **Email address** — your login credential and the address for all notifications
    - **Password** — minimum 8 characters; use a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols

    After submitting the form, check your inbox for a verification email from **hello@inkbot.io** and click the confirmation link. Your account is active as soon as your email is verified.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Upload Your Design">
    From your **Dashboard**, navigate to **Designs → Upload** and select your artwork file.

    Accepted formats and requirements:

    | Format | Max Size | Notes |
    |--------|----------|-------|
    | SVG    | 50 MB    | Recommended for vector art; preserves crisp edges at any scale |
    | PNG    | 50 MB    | Use the highest resolution available; minimum 300 DPI for print quality |
    | PDF    | 50 MB    | Single-page only; embed all fonts and flatten transparency before uploading |

    Inkbot automatically checks your file for print compatibility — color space, resolution, and line weight. Any issues are flagged immediately with guidance on how to fix them.

    <Tip>
      Before uploading, review the [Design Guidelines](/design/design-guidelines) for best practices on preparing your artwork, including recommended canvas sizes, color modes, and minimum stroke widths.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Preview Your Design on Your Body">
    Open the **3D Body Map** tool from your Dashboard or directly from your uploaded design's detail page.

    Follow these steps to set your placement:

    1. **Choose a body zone** — select the broad area (e.g., forearm, upper back, calf) from the body diagram.
    2. **Position the design** — drag it to your desired location on the 3D model.
    3. **Scale and rotate** — use the on-screen controls or pinch/twist gestures to resize and orient the design until it fits naturally.
    4. **Inspect from all angles** — rotate the 3D model to review how the design wraps around curves and contours.
    5. **Save your placement** — click **Save Placement** to lock in the configuration. You can save multiple placements and compare them before booking.

    Your saved placement is automatically attached to your session booking in the next step.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Book Your Studio Session">
    From your **Dashboard**, go to **Book Session** and complete your booking:

    1. **Select a studio** — choose from the list of certified Inkbot studios near you. Each listing shows availability, distance, and technician ratings.
    2. **Pick a date and time** — available slots are shown in your local time zone. Slots marked **Express** are optimized for smaller designs (under 10 cm²).
    3. **Attach your design** — confirm which saved design and placement configuration to use for the session.
    4. **Confirm your booking** — review the session summary and click **Confirm**. You'll receive a booking confirmation email within a few minutes.

    Your confirmation email includes the studio address, session duration estimate, a checklist of what to bring, and a link to reschedule if needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>
