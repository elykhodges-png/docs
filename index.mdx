Inkbot is the world's first robotic tattoo printing system. Using advanced 3D body-scanning technology, Inkbot maps your skin surface, scales and orients your chosen design to fit perfectly, and then applies it with sub-millimeter robotic precision — no guesswork, no freehand variation.

## Get started in four steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Sign up at [inkbot.io](https://inkbot.io) to access the design studio and booking system.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload or create your design">
    Import your own artwork or use the built-in design tools. Inkbot accepts SVG, PNG, and PDF files.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview on your body">
    Use the 3D body-mapping tool to position, scale, and preview your design exactly where you want it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Book your session">
    Choose a studio location and time slot. Bring your confirmed booking to the studio and let Inkbot do the rest.
  </Step>
</Steps>
