Inkbot is a 3D body-printing system built for tattoos. A full-body scanner maps your skin topology in three dimensions; a robotic arm then applies ink with sub-millimeter precision. Upload your artwork, position it on an interactive 3D body model, and book a session at a certified Inkbot studio — the machine handles the rest.
