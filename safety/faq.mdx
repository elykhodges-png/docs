## General

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Is Inkbot a real tattoo?">
    Yes. Inkbot produces a permanent tattoo using the same ink and needle technology found in traditional tattooing. The ink is deposited into the dermis layer of your skin, just as it would be by a human tattoo artist. The result is a permanent, fade-resistant tattoo that requires the same aftercare as any other tattoo.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How is Inkbot different from a human tattoo artist?">
    Inkbot's robotic system delivers precision and repeatability that is difficult to achieve by hand. The 3D body-mapping technology maps the exact contours of your skin before printing, allowing the needle array to maintain consistent depth and spacing across the entire design. Every pass is logged and executed to sub-millimeter accuracy.

    That said, Inkbot does not replace the creative and artistic judgment of a skilled tattoo artist. Inkbot is a printing system — it executes your design exactly as submitted. If you are looking for freehand artistry, custom illustration, or collaborative creative development, we recommend working with a human artist and using Inkbot for reproduction or precision work.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is Inkbot available in my city?">
    Inkbot studios are expanding rapidly. Use the [studio locator at inkbot.io/studios](https://inkbot.io/studios) to find the nearest location to you, check opening hours, and browse available appointment slots.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How much does a session cost?">
    Pricing starts at **$149 for small designs**. The total cost of your session depends on the size, complexity, and color count of your design. Visit [inkbot.io/pricing](https://inkbot.io/pricing) for the full pricing breakdown, including details on multi-session discounts and touch-up rates.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Safety

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Does it hurt?">
    The sensation is similar to traditional tattooing — most customers describe it as a scratching or vibrating feeling. Pain tolerance varies by person and by location on the body. Areas over bone (ribs, ankles, spine) tend to be more sensitive than fleshy areas.

    Numbing cream is available at all Inkbot studios. Request it when booking via **Dashboard → Book Session → Special Requests**, or ask your studio technician on arrival. Allow 30–45 minutes for the numbing cream to take effect before the session begins.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Are the inks safe?">
    Yes. All Inkbot inks are FDA-cleared, hypoallergenic by default, and free of heavy metals including nickel, mercury, and lead. They are also vegan-friendly and contain no animal-derived ingredients.

    If you have a history of skin sensitivities or previous reactions to tattoo ink, request a patch test when booking. Learn more on the [Skin Safety](/safety/skin-safety) page.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I get a tattoo if I have sensitive skin?">
    Many customers with sensitive skin have successful sessions with Inkbot. However, we recommend booking a patch test before your full session if you have a history of reactions to cosmetic products, dyes, or previous tattoo ink. You can request a patch test via **Dashboard → Book Session → Special Requests**.

    If you have an active skin condition such as eczema, psoriasis, or dermatitis, do not book a session until the affected area has fully cleared. When in doubt, consult your dermatologist before booking.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if I'm pregnant?">
    Inkbot sessions are not available during pregnancy or breastfeeding. Tattooing during pregnancy carries risks to both the parent and the developing baby, including infection risk and potential ink absorption. Please wait until after you have finished breastfeeding before booking a session, and consult your physician if you have questions.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Design & Booking

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What file formats are accepted?">
    Inkbot accepts designs in the following formats:

    - **SVG** (recommended for vector artwork — best for clean lines and scalability)
    - **PNG** (minimum 300 DPI at print size)
    - **PDF** (single-page, flattened)

    For full guidance on preparing your design for the best possible print result, see the [Design Guidelines](/design/design-guidelines) page.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I change my design after booking?">
    Yes. You can update your design up to **12 hours before your session** using the Inkbot app. Go to **Dashboard → Orders**, select your upcoming session, and tap **Edit Design**. Changes submitted less than 12 hours before the session start time cannot be accepted, as the design is locked for pre-session processing.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How far in advance do I need to book?">
    Most Inkbot studios have appointment availability within **1–2 weeks**. High-demand locations in major cities may require **2–3 weeks** of lead time, particularly on weekends. We recommend booking as early as possible to secure your preferred time slot. Check real-time availability at [inkbot.io/studios](https://inkbot.io/studios).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I bring a friend to my session?">
    Yes. You are welcome to bring **one support person** with you to the studio. They will be allowed in the session room with you throughout the process. For space and safety reasons, additional guests beyond one are not permitted in the session area, though they are welcome to wait in the studio reception.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## After Your Session

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How long does it take to heal?">
    Full healing typically takes **2–4 weeks**, though this varies by individual skin type, the size of the tattoo, and the body location. During the first few days, the area will be tender and may appear slightly red or swollen — this is normal. Follow the aftercare instructions provided by your studio technician, and refer to the Aftercare guide in the app for day-by-day guidance.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I touch up my tattoo?">
    Yes. Touch-up sessions are available at any Inkbot studio, not just the one where you had your original session. Touch-ups are priced separately from full sessions. Log in to the app and select **Book a Touch-Up** to see pricing and availability.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if I don't like the result?">
    If your tattoo does not match the approved design due to a printing error, contact Inkbot support within **7 days** of your session. Include clear photos of the affected area and your session order number. Inkbot offers a **free correction session** for verified printing errors.

    Note that dissatisfaction with a design you submitted and approved — including color, size, or placement — does not qualify for a free correction. We strongly recommend using the 3D preview tool during booking to review your design on a body map before confirming.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
