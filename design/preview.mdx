---

Before any ink is applied, Inkbot gives you a photorealistic mockup of your design rendered directly onto your 3D body model. The preview step is your opportunity to fine-tune placement, check scale, experiment with orientation, and feel genuinely confident about the result — all before you commit to a print session. Take as much time as you need here; changes in the preview tool cost nothing and take seconds.

## Opening the preview tool

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to your design">
    From your Inkbot dashboard, go to **Dashboard → Designs** and select the design you want to preview.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch the preview">
    Click **Preview on Body** in the design detail panel. The preview tool opens in a full-screen view.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a body zone">
    Select the body zone where you intend to place the tattoo (for example, left upper arm, right calf, upper back). This determines which region of your body model is shown.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Load your body scan or generic model">
    If you have a saved 3D body scan on file, it loads automatically and the preview renders on your actual skin surface geometry. If no scan is saved yet, Inkbot loads a calibrated generic body model in the closest available size and shape profile. You can complete a real body scan at the studio before your session for maximum accuracy.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Position and adjust your design">
    Drag, scale, rotate, and mirror the design overlay on the body model until the placement looks exactly right. All controls are described in the section below.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Preview controls

Use the following controls inside the preview tool to adjust your design's placement and appearance.

| Control | Action |
|---------|--------|
| Drag | Move the design across the skin surface |
| Pinch / scroll | Scale the design up or down |
| Rotate handle | Rotate the design around its centre point |
| Mirror toggle | Flip the design horizontally |
| Opacity slider | Blend the design with the skin tone for a more realistic healed appearance |

<Tip>
  Try multiple placements before settling on one. Use the **Save Placement** button to snapshot each option — you can save up to five placement variations and compare them side by side before deciding which to send to the studio.
</Tip>

## Saving a placement

When you are happy with a placement, click **Save Placement** in the preview toolbar. Inkbot saves your design position, scale, rotation, and mirror state as a **Print Configuration**.

The Print Configuration is automatically linked to your design and sent to the studio system when you book a print session. Your technician can pull it up on the studio display to confirm everything looks correct before the robotic arm begins.

To view, rename, or delete saved Print Configurations, go to **Dashboard → Designs → [your design] → Print Configurations**.

<Info>
  The final printed position may vary by up to **2 mm** from the preview due to natural skin movement between the time of your body scan and the moment of printing. Your technician will confirm positioning with you immediately before the session begins, and minor on-the-spot adjustments can be made through the studio interface at that point.
</Info>
