Inkbot is a next-generation tattoo service that combines 3D body-scanning technology with a robotic printing arm to apply your custom designs directly onto your skin — precisely, safely, and repeatably. Whether you're getting your first tattoo or your fiftieth, Inkbot removes the guesswork from placement, size, and symmetry, giving you a studio experience grounded in technology and confidence.

## What Makes Inkbot Different

Inkbot isn't just a tattoo studio — it's a precision platform. Here's what sets it apart from traditional tattooing:

## Who Uses Inkbot

Inkbot is designed for anyone who values precision and wants full creative control over their tattoo experience:

- **First-timers** who want to see exactly how a design will look before committing to it permanently.
- **Tattoo enthusiasts** building sleeves or body compositions that require perfect alignment across multiple sessions.
- **Detail-oriented clients** who need exact placement — think geometric patterns, portraits, or text that must sit level on a specific contour.
- **Artists and designers** who want their digital artwork translated to skin with zero interpretation loss.

<Note>
  Inkbot sessions are available exclusively at certified Inkbot studios. All equipment is operated by trained Inkbot technicians. Find your nearest certified studio at [inkbot.io/studios](https://inkbot.io/studios).
</Note>
