Inkbot turns your digital artwork into a finished tattoo through a seamless, technology-driven process — all within a single studio session. From the moment you upload your design to the moment you walk out with fresh ink, every step is guided by 3D body-mapping data and executed by a calibrated robotic arm. Here's exactly how it works.

<Info>
  A typical Inkbot session lasts between **30 and 90 minutes**, depending on the size and complexity of your design. Large or highly detailed pieces may require a follow-up session for color fills or touch-ups.
</Info>

## The Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Account and Upload Your Design">
    Start at [inkbot.io/signup](https://inkbot.io/signup) to create your free Inkbot account. Once logged in, navigate to **Dashboard → Designs → Upload** and submit your artwork.

    Accepted file formats:
    - **SVG** — recommended for vector artwork; scales without quality loss
    - **PNG** — up to 50 MB; use the highest resolution available
    - **PDF** — single-page documents with embedded vector or raster art

    After uploading, Inkbot automatically analyzes your file for print compatibility and flags any resolution or color-space issues before your session.
  </Step>

  <Step title="3D Body Scan at the Studio">
    When you arrive at your certified Inkbot studio, a technician will guide you through a full or partial 3D body scan using Inkbot's structured-light scanner. The scan takes **under two minutes** and captures your skin's precise topology — every curve, contour, and surface angle in the target area.

    The resulting 3D mesh is linked to your account and used to anchor your design placement with sub-millimeter accuracy during printing.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Position Your Design with the Preview Tool">
    Using the **3D Body Map tool** — available on the studio's tablet or your own device — you place your uploaded design onto the scanned model of your body. You can:

    - **Drag** the design to any position on the target area
    - **Scale** it up or down using pinch gestures or the size controls
    - **Rotate** it to follow the natural lines of your body
    - **Preview** it from any angle with the 360° view

    Once you're satisfied with the placement, save your configuration. The studio technician reviews it with you before proceeding.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Pre-Print Safety Check">
    Before the robotic arm begins, Inkbot's system runs an automated **pre-print safety check** that verifies:

    - Equipment calibration is within tolerance
    - Ink cartridges are fully loaded and sealed
    - The scan-to-placement mapping has no geometric errors
    - The target skin area is free of obstructions (hair, debris, or moisture)

    The technician confirms the results and obtains your final consent. You can make last-minute adjustments to placement or cancel at no charge at this stage.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Robotic Printing">
    With safety checks complete, the robotic arm begins printing. It follows the 3D-mapped path calculated from your body scan, applying ink in precise, controlled passes. The arm adjusts dynamically for skin curvature so lines remain consistent across contoured surfaces.

    You'll feel a sensation similar to traditional tattooing. The technician monitors the process in real time and can pause or stop at any point.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Post-Print Review and Aftercare">
    When printing is complete, the technician photographs the finished tattoo and uploads it to your account for your records. Together, you review the result against your original design preview.

    Before you leave, you'll receive:

    - A printed and digital **aftercare instruction sheet** tailored to your skin type and ink coverage
    - A follow-up appointment option if touch-ups are needed
    - Access to your session summary in your Inkbot dashboard, including your 3D scan, placement file, and print log
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Arrive at your session with the target skin area **clean and well-moisturized** (but not oily). Healthy, hydrated skin improves ink adhesion and produces sharper lines. Avoid sunburn, fresh exfoliation, or heavy lotion application on the day of your session.
</Tip>
