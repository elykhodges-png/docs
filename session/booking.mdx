Inkbot sessions are available exclusively at certified studio locations and must be booked in advance through the Inkbot web app at [inkbot.io](https://inkbot.io) or the Inkbot mobile app. Walk-ins are not accepted. The booking system lets you select your saved design configuration, browse available studios near you, and lock in a time slot that works for your schedule — all in just a few minutes.

## Before you book

Make sure you've taken care of the following before starting the booking flow:

- **Your design is ready.** Your design must be uploaded and a Print Configuration saved before you can book. [Finalize your design and save a Print Configuration →](/design/preview)
- **You meet the age requirement.** You must be 18 or older to book independently. Customers aged 16–17 may attend with a parent or legal guardian present and consenting at the studio.
- **Your skin is in good condition.** The area you plan to have tattooed should be free of open wounds, active rashes, eczema flare-ups, or recent sunburn. If you're unsure, consult a dermatologist before booking.

## How to book

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in and open the booking flow">
    Sign in to your Inkbot account and navigate to **Dashboard → Book Session**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your Print Configuration">
    Choose the saved Print Configuration you want to use for this session. Only finalized configurations are eligible for booking.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a studio">
    Browse available Inkbot-certified studios using the interactive map or the list view. Use the filters to narrow results by city, distance, or availability.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a date and time slot">
    Select a date from the calendar and choose an open time slot. Slots are displayed in your local time zone.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and confirm">
    Check your session summary — studio location, date, time, and selected Print Configuration — then tap **Confirm Booking**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive your confirmation">
    You'll receive a confirmation email within a few minutes containing your session details and a **QR code** to present at the studio. Save it to your phone or print it out.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Cancellations and rescheduling

You can cancel or reschedule your session at no charge up to **24 hours before your appointment start time**. Cancellations or reschedules made within 24 hours of the appointment are subject to a **$25 fee**. Customers who do not show up for their appointment without prior cancellation **forfeit the full session fee**. To cancel or reschedule, go to **Dashboard → My Sessions** and select the relevant booking.

<Warning>
  Cancellations and reschedules must be made at least **24 hours in advance** to avoid fees. Changes made inside the 24-hour window incur a **$25 fee**, and no-shows forfeit the full session fee. Plan ahead to avoid unexpected charges.
</Warning>

<Info>
  **What to bring to your session:**
  - Your **booking confirmation QR code** (email or screenshot on your phone)
  - A **valid government-issued photo ID** (driver's license, passport, or equivalent)
  - Any **special placement notes** you'd like to discuss with the technician before printing begins
</Info>
